Specific instance:
$$\textcolor{Golden}{f(x) = 2x^2 + 3x + 1}$$

\textcolor{SkyBlue}{Calculation:}
\begin{align*}
f'(x) &= 4x + 3 \\
f'(0) &= 3 \\
f''(x) &= 4
\end{align*}

Result: Minimum at $x = -\frac{3}{4}$
